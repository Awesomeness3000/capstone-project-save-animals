    Endangered

    Dolphins are one of the many endangered species of the world.
    They are marine animals which means they live in the sea.
    The reason dolphins are endangered is because the sea is getting polluted.
    You can help though! Don't throw your trash in the sea and help in
    charities. Even simplest things like that can help.
